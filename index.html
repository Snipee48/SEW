About SEW

Your Trusted Cold Storage Partner

Sivasankar Electrical Works (SEW) is a leading provider of comprehensive AC solutions and cold storage room development across Tamil Nadu. With years of expertise and a commitment to excellence, we deliver state-of-the-art cooling solutions for commercial and industrial applications.
